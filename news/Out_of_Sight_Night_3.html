The Michigan Council of the Blind and Visually Impaired

Is Please to Announce

Out of Sight Night!

A Celebration of “Living Without Limits”

Enjoy a great dinner, and entertainment by the great band

Beyond Sight



Thursday September 25, 2014

Four Points by Sheraton

3600 East Cork Street, Kalamazoo

Just off Sprinkle Road at I-94

6:00 – 8:00 Deluxe gourmet pizza and salad buffet

7:00 – 10:00 Beyond Sight



Come and enjoy a great night of food and entertainment and support the work of the Michigan Council of the Blind and Visually Impaired (MCBVI), the Michigan affiliate of the American Council of the Blind! We are working together to enhance the lives of all Michigan Citizens with vision loss. Your $45 ticket includes dinner and entertainment! A cash bar will be available. Beyond Sight is a great variety band with a huge fan base! The 4 piece band includes 2 members who are blind! Find them on FaceBook at www.facebook.com/beyondsightMI. Come on and enjoy great entertainment with MCBVI and Beyond Sight at Out of Sight Night!



Please make reservations by September 15 by calling 616-534-0581 (before 9pm please)

If your business would like to sponsor Out of Sight Night and the MCBVI convention, contact president Joe Sibley at 616-724-1650 or joe.sibley@comcast.net! Sponsorships are available at many levels, or you could be the title sponsor for Out of Sight Night!
