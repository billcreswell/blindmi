Convention Update

Published April 2013

Plans are falling into place for the 2013 convention of the Michigan Council of the Blind and Visually Impaired!

Plan to join us September 12-15 at the Best Western Plus in Lansing!

Our theme this year is: “Changing Lives, Changing Times, A Positive Attitude Makes A Diference!”

We will have exhibits, educational sessions, Fun activities, the annual auction, and much much more!

At our banquet Saturday night we will have fun with Gerry Grossman, the Human Jukebox! Gerry is a great comedian from Chicago who has worked with some of the biggest names in entertainment for many years! You can learn more about Gerry at: http://www.humanjukeboxx.com Note that there are two x at the end.

Watch the convention page for more details and registration information!

Don't miss the fun, education, and fellowship at the 2013 MCBVI convention!
